About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and AI risk in modern organisations. Through collaboration with UK universities and industry partners, IDR bridges academic insight with real-world projects to develop professionals capable of managing complex risk environments.

At IDR, learning goes beyond traditional classroom education. The institute emphasizes hands-on training, practical case studies, and real industry scenarios to prepare individuals for the evolving challenges of digital risk management. By combining academic research with industry expertise, IDR equips professionals with the skills needed to identify, assess, and mitigate risks related to cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, data governance, and digital transformation.

With a strong focus on innovation and collaboration, IDR aims to build a new generation of professionals who can help organisations operate securely, responsibly, and confidently in an increasingly digital world.

Community

IDR serves students, early-career professionals, and organisations in sectors such as financial services and critical infrastructure seeking to strengthen digital and cyber risk capabilities. By bringing together learners, industry experts, and technology leaders, IDR creates a collaborative community focused on addressing modern digital challenges.

Through mentorship, training programs, and knowledge-sharing initiatives, the institute helps individuals and organisations develop practical skills and stay ahead of evolving cyber and AI-related risks. This community-driven approach enables members to learn from real-world experiences, exchange insights, and contribute to building safer and more resilient digital ecosystems.
